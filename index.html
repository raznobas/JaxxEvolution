How to fund?

It's easy to top up any cryptocurrency account in Jaxx.

Receive

Go to the page of the desired cryptocurrency from the 'Wallet' section and click on the 'Receive' button.

Copy

Copy the wallet address of the digital coin you want to deposit or use the QR code.

Paste

When processing a transaction in the Jaxx wallet, paste the copied address of the cryptocurrency wallet into the designated field in the transaction request.

Confirm

Confirm the transfer, and you're done!
